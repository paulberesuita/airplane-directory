## How to Download Images

1. Visit the Wikimedia category link
2. Click on an image you like
3. Click "Download" or right-click and save
4. Rename to match the expected filename (e.g., `boeing-737-800.jpg`)
5. Place in the correct airline folder under `images/airlines/`

## Image Requirements

- **Format:** JPG preferred
- **Size:** At least 1200px wide recommended
- **Orientation:** Landscape (horizontal)
- **Content:** Clear view of the aircraft with airline livery visible
- **License:** CC-BY-SA or more permissive

## Attribution

When using Wikimedia Commons images, include attribution. Add to site footer or about page:
> Aircraft photos from Wikimedia Commons contributors, licensed under CC BY-SA.

## Alternative Sources

If Wikimedia doesn't have a specific image:

1. **Unsplash** - https://unsplash.com/s/photos/airplane (Free, no attribution required)
2. **Airline Press Kits** - Often have downloadable media
   - [American Airlines Newsroom](https://news.aa.com/)
   - [Delta News Hub](https://news.delta.com/)
   - [United Newsroom](https://www.united.com/newsroom)
   - [Southwest Newsroom](https://www.swamedia.com/)
